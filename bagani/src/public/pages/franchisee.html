FAQ'S

Most frequent questions and answers

I DON'T HAVE A DEDICATED STORE, HOW WILL I RUN THE BUSINESS?

You can easily just get us the orders via the website or contacting us and we can take that further. No dedicated store is required if you opt for the Add-on Franchise!

I DON'T KNOW MUCH ABOUT THIS BUSINESS AND THEIR OPERATIONS, HOW WILL I INITIATE?

Don’t worry! We are here. We will provide you the Training and Induction to guide through all the necessary steps to run the Franchise of Chhapai.com

WHAT IS THE PROFIT MARGIN FOR ME?

Your profit margin will be somewhere between 22% to 45%, the percentage varies with the type of Chhapai products like business cards, show-pieces or any other accessories.

WHAT EQUIPMENTS ARE USED TO SET-UP THE FRANCHISE?

All you need is a computer to keep a check on your franchise business, and proofing printers to get prints of your designs for approval. And nothing more!

CAN I ALLOW A DISCOUNT TO MY CLIENTS?

Yes, you can allow a discount to your clients completely at your ease. The discount allowed to your client shall be from your profit margin.

CAN I PITCH A HIGHER PRICE TO MY CLIENT?

No, we request you to not pitch a price higher than that on the website to your clients. It would be good to keep the price structure fixed to avoid any misguidance.
